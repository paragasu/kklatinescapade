Artists

We bring you the best salsa, bachata and kizomba dancers



Azlan Mckechnie

The quickest way to improve is to dance with those that inspire you - Azlan

Azlan originally from the United Kingdom, was Introduced to Kizomba in 2006 at a workshop by Kwenda Lima. Inspired by the music and the emphasis on connection, he has worked to express himself through this dance both socially and choreographically.

As an instructor, he has taught various dance styles to students as well as other instructors all around the world including the UK, Africa, Australia and Asia. He is also credited with pioneering and growing the Kizomba scene in Malaysia since moving there in 2011.

Known for his humour and high energy in class, Azlan’s classes are always fun from beginner to advanced level. His ability to break down the technique, allows even the most inexperienced dancer to grasp the fundamentals of the dance, armed with the tools to improve their dancing outside of the class.

Davis Dave

To identify Dave Davis Vainqueur, he comes from the Caribbean islands, where the music is present in every street corner so Dave is passionate by music.

He began his career at 6 years old, as a dancer and singer in a carnival group AKYO, with his father behind him as a musician. Then a few years later, he did his first dance show. During his childhood he has been influenced by so many styles especially on the dance floor.

His favorite dance remains the Caribbean 's zouk! Expatriate in France for his professional sports's career he decided to take Salsa, Bachata and Kizomba's lessons in order to improve himself. Kizomba which is very close to Zouk became naturally his style and passion, he decided to share this with the Hong Kong community.

Dave will bring you: musicality, sensuality and good vibes.

Artia Zandian

Winner of numerous salsa championships and founder of the "Shines Brothers", and "New Blood" professional dance teams, Artia was born in the capital city of Tehran/Iran.

He chased his dream to dance out of Iran were dancing was only done behind closed doors. Artia has now been travelling and sharing his unique style as well as his love and passion for Latin dancing around the world. He is one of the most regarded upcoming professionals in the Australian scene and is sought after for his style, energy, teaching and social dancing all around the world.

1st place in the World Solo category at 2017 World Salsa Championship in Hong Kong, 3rd place in World Latin Dance Cup.1st place in the Professional Solo and Couple categories at the Salsa solo Championships in Brisbane.

Artia won 1st place for various Australian Championship titles in the Solo, Team, and Partnered categories. He has studied Latin dancing and been mentored by some of Australia and worlds best including 8 time world champion Oliver Pineda and world renown teacher choreographer and promoter Jaime Jesus Manuelian. He also trained in ballet, lyrical, hip-hop, and jazz with Robert Sturrock and the Industry Dance Academy, and was a member of the elite “Modus Vivendi” salsa team competing in world level competitions.

Amar Singh

Amar is the founder, instructor and choreographer at Dance Blaze Academy. His one Mission is to spread the joy of dance not just across Malaysia but the rest of the world.

Amar’s early experience in the performing art of dance commenced in the non-Latin genres of Bhangra and Hip hop. Amar started teaching Cuban Salsa and Bachata in 2005 and has been adding on different dances to his repertoire with Dominican Bachata, Reggaeton, Afro Cuban dances, and his famous body movement classes.

A crowd favorite in the Malaysian social salsa circuit, Amar continues to wow the masses with his freestyle and groovy dance moves.

Over the years Amar has not just been recognized as a leading figure in the Malaysian dance industry but highly sought after Internationally as well with his performing and teaching tours all the way across South East Asia ,Germany, Holland, UK, Japan, India.

His outlook towards dance has evolved as he draws a parallel between life relationships and dance. Enriching different aspects of ones life.

A genuine desire to see individuals grow not just as better dancers but as better individuals through this parallel has made him one of the most respected and sought-after instructors and dancers.

DJ Selva

"DJ Selva is one of the most sought after Afro-Latin DJs and Emcees in Asia.

Reputed as the prodigal “crowd-pleaser”, his early training in classical music from the age of 6 and experience as a DJ in R&B dance clubs from the tender age of 17 has helped him gain an invaluable skill in the art of music-mixing and crowd-pleasing. Coupled with outstanding work ethic, DJ Selva has established himself as one of the biggest names in the Asian Afro-Latin industry. He is regularly invited to share his music at some of the biggest festivals and congresses, equally renowned for his mixes in Kizomba as he is in Salsa, Bachata & Zouk.

DJ Selva was given due recognition as a world-class Afro-Latin DJ when he was invited to be the resident DJ at the World Bachata Festival 2012 in Kuala Lumpur. He has since been invited to numerous events alongside some of the best Afro-Latin DJs in the region in many different countries including Singapore, Thailand, Indonesia, Phillippines, Vietnam, India, East Timor & Japan.

DJ Selva’s introduction to Kizomba in 2011 and many years of practice thereafter has lead to his status as one of the most popular Kizomba DJs in Asia. To his credit, he has been invited to DJ at many events featuring some of the biggest artistes in world Kizomba including Kwenda Lima, Albir Rojas, Sara Lopez, Sara Panero, Laurent Yishu, Adeline Sudois, Felicien & Isabelle Rossa, Isabelle Crepin, Helio Santos, Ronie Saleh, Enah Lebon, etc.
